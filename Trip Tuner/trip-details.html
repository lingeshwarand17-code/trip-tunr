Tokyo City Exploration

4.9 Rating 342 Reviews
Tokyo, Japan Asia
8 Days 7 Nights
Immerse yourself in the electrifying energy of Tokyo! This 8-day exploration blends Japan's cutting-edge modernity with its profound ancient traditions. From the neon-lit streets of Shinjuku and the bustling Shibuya Crossing to the serene grounds of Meiji Shrine and historic Asakusa, experience the best of both worlds. Savor world-class sushi, discover hidden izakayas, and enjoy breathtaking views from the Tokyo Skytree.

Itinerary

Arrival in Tokyo Day 1

Arrival at Narita/Haneda Airport. Airport transfer to your central Tokyo hotel. Evening welcome dinner in Shinjuku.

Traditional Tokyo: Asakusa & Ueno Day 2

Visit the historic Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa. Explore traditional craft shops down Nakamise street. Afternoon visit to Ueno Park and the Tokyo National Museum.

Modern Marvels: Shibuya & Harajuku Day 3

Experience the famous Shibuya Crossing. Walk through vibrant Takeshita Street in Harajuku. Visit the tranquil Meiji Shrine surrounded by forest.

Free Day & Departure Day 8

Morning free for last-minute shopping or Tsukiji outer market visit. Transfer back to the airport.
